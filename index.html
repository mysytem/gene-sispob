Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To serve the residents of Barangay Poblacion Pitogo with integrity, transparency, and efficiency. We are committed to delivering quality public service, promoting community welfare, and fostering sustainable development through active citizen participation and good governance.

Our Vision

A progressive, peaceful, and resilient Barangay Poblacion Pitogo where residents enjoy a high quality of life, equal opportunities, and a strong sense of community. We envision a barangay that is a model of effective local governance and sustainable development in Zamboanga del Sur.

Our Services

Barangay Clearance

Issuance of barangay clearance for various transactions and requirements.

ID & Certifications

Processing of barangay ID, indigency certificates, and other documents.

Dispute Resolution

Mediation and settlement of community disputes through the Lupong Tagapamayapa.

Health Services

Coordination with local health centers for medical missions and health programs.
